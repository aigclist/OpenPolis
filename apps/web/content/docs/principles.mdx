# Project Principles

## Task-structured information

Information is not stored for reading alone. Every object should be routable into assignment, review, execution, and reporting flows.

## Agent-native, not agent-led

Agents can summarize, classify, draft, and flag risk. Agents cannot publish, decide political posture, or bypass a human reviewer.

## Objects over pages

The system is built around structured operational objects such as issues, briefs, assets, tasks, events, feedback, and approvals.

## Auditability as a first-class capability

Every material action should be attributable: who created it, who edited it, which agent assisted, who reviewed it, and who exported it.
